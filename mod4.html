Can modernization and technology abolish political dynasty? 

Modernization and technology, particularly in the form of automated elections, have been seen as potential tools to modernize democracy and potentially disrupt political dynasties.

However, the experience in the Philippines, a country with a strong presence of political dynasties, suggests that technology alone may not be sufficient to abolish political dynasties. Despite the introduction of automated elections, the number of political dynasties occupying government seats increased, suggesting that the power equation in the country was not significantly altered by the infusion of modern technology.
